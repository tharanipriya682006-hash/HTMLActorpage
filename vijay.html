Vijay

Vijay is an Indian actor and playback singer who works in Tamil cinema. He has won numerous awards, including four Tamil Nadu State Film Awards, three Filmfare Awards South, and a National Film Award. He is one of the highest-paid actors in Tamil cinema and has a large fan following.

Vijay made his acting debut as a child artist in the film "Vetri" (1984) and later made his debut as a lead actor in the film "Naalaiya Theerpu" (1992). He gained popularity with his performances in films like "Poove Unakkaga" (1996), "Kadhalukku Mariyadhai" (1997), and "Thulladha Manamum Thullum" (1999). He has since starred in several successful films, including "Ghilli" (2004), "Thuppakki" (2012), and "Mersal" (2017).
